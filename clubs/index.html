Clubs

Two ways to bring CyberNGO to your community.

Start a CyberNGO chapter from scratch — or affiliate the club you already run with our awareness mission.

🎓

Start a CyberNGO Club

Launch a brand-new CyberNGO chapter at your college, community, or city. You'll run awareness drives, victim help sessions, and CyberNGO-branded events under our mentorship.

  • ▸Official CyberNGO chapter under our brand
  • ▸Monthly mentorship from the core CyberNGO team
  • ▸Awareness materials, talk decks, and certificates
  • ▸Direct line to law-enforcement contacts (with supervision)
Start a chapter →
🤝

Register your existing club

Already run a tech / cyber-security / InfoSec club? Affiliate with CyberNGO for joint events, shared resources, and visibility — without giving up your own identity.

  • ▸Keep your own club name and brand
  • ▸Co-host awareness workshops
  • ▸Cross-promotion on CyberNGO channels
  • ▸Listed in our partner directory
Register your club →

About Our NGO

CyberNGO helps people fight against Cyber Crime and understand the pain and concern of Cyber-crime victims. We help and guide people to fight against Cyber Crime — because being a victim of crime and fighting against it is not easy.
